How It Works
  1. Fill in the recipient's email address
  2. Enter your name and email address
  3. Add a subject and your message
  4. Optionally attach a file (max 5MB)
  5. Click "Send Email"

Note: This uses EmailJS to send emails. You'll need to set up an EmailJS account and configure the service with your credentials.
